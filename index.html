Introduction


Like the importance of education and healthcare expenditures, the number of crimes is also a significant factor which may indicate a situation and development of an area, since it may have a relationship with economy (or GDP), population, or unemployment rate within a particular area.

In this final project, I will discuss what factors might influence crime rates from 1970 to present through U.S. states. Those factors are categorized into demographic, economic and social aspects. Also, the datasets used in this project can be found from the "ABOUT" web page.
